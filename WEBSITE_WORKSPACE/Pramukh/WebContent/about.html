About Us

We bring you custom luxury menswear at revolutionary prices; direct from one of the finest makers in the world.

We definitely do not believe in the one-size-fits-all policy, and understand that each individual is unique. Our state-of-the-art cutting technology allows us to generate and store your individual patterns in order to re-create the exact fit each time you make a purchase. No more multiple visits to your local tailor to adjust your clothes and no more compromises required with ready to wear, ill fitting off the rack garments. Our level of fit and finish will leave you more than satisfied every time.
