PRAMUKH TAILOR

We believe that your clothes should be made to fit you no matter what size you are. We makes clothes for your body type,to suit your unique shape and size - experience the pleasure of the fit you have always dream of!

MATERIALS

Pramukh insist on only using the finest fabrics such as high thread and other material carefully selected from the finest mills, Our Garments are crafted of fabrics that will give you the best qaulity , color, shape and out.

DETAILING

At Pramukh, we have an eye for the finest detail. We use real urea and lead buttons for durability, Germen shoulder pads for the right shape and form and even wrapped shank buttons using the latest technology to avoid it popping off. We provide generous seam allowance for future adjusting. Pramukh take care of all little things, so don't you have to worry about it.

CONSTRUCTION

The Secrets of good suits lies in construction. We are proud to craft suits that are just as amazing internally as they are externally. Pramukh Suits, whether with 3 ply floating chest piece or roll-padded half or full canvas, uses superior German and Italian interlining; So that your garment drapes well and retain its shapes to fit you just perfectly forever. Our pants are made with a revolutionary curved waist band and our side adjuster buckle will provide you with that perfect fit.Every garment is pre-inspected and quality controlled several times over before it leaves us.
